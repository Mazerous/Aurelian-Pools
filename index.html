About Aurelian Pools

Built on discipline, pride, and customer-first service.

Hi, I’m Austin Molketin, the owner and operator of Aurelian Pools. I graduated from the University of South Florida (USF) in 2024 with a degree in Computer Science. As AI rapidly reshaped the industry and opportunities narrowed, I made a decision: pivot, work harder, and build something I could stand behind.

Today, I run Aurelian Pools full-time and I’m a Certified Pool Operator (CPO). This business is personal. When you hire Aurelian Pools, you’re getting an owner who shows up, communicates clearly, and treats your home with respect. No corners cut. No excuses.

FAQ

Quick answers.

What’s included in “equipment checks”?
Each visit includes a visual + functional check of your pump, filter pressure, timers, and obvious leaks. If something looks off, you’ll get a clear explanation and a recommendation before any repair work.
Do you send updates after service?
Yes. After every visit we send chemical readings and pictures via email, plus on-the-way notifications so you know when we’re arriving.
What services don’t we cover?
We focus on keeping your pool clean and swim-ready—no distractions. We do not handle major equipment replacement (pump replacements, heaters), plumbing, leak detection/repair, or pool construction/resurfacing. If you need those, we’ll recommend a trusted specialist.
What is water polishing?
It’s a premium clarity process for that “sparkle” look—fine-tuned chemistry (like clarifier as needed), optimized filtration, and extra detail cleaning to remove micro-particles that cause dull or hazy water.
How do I start service?
Send a request through the quote form below. We’ll confirm pool type, access, and a start date—then we get you on a consistent weekly schedule.
