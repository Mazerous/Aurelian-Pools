About Aurelian Pools

Built on discipline, pride, and customer-first service.

Hi, I’m Austin Molketin, the owner and operator of Aurelian Pools. I graduated from the University of South Florida (USF) in 2024 with a B.S. in Computer Science. As AI rapidly reshaped the industry and opportunities narrowed, I made a decision: pivot, work harder, and build something I could stand behind.

I started servicing pools part-time in 2023, and before going full-time I worked at three different pool companies—a small/local company, a mid-size operation, and a large corporate route. I saw the business at every level.

The pattern I kept seeing was the same: customers were rushed, communication was poor, and the focus was on making as much money as possible instead of caring for the pool and the homeowner. That never sat right with me.

Today, I run Aurelian Pools full-time and I’m a Certified Pool Operator (CPO). When you hire Aurelian Pools, you’re getting an owner who shows up, communicates clearly, and treats your home with respect. No corners cut. No excuses.

FAQ

Quick answers.

What’s included in “equipment checks”?
Each visit includes a visual + functional check of your pump, filter pressure, timers, and obvious leaks. If something looks off, you’ll get a clear explanation and a recommendation before any repair work.
Do you send updates after service?
Yes. After every visit we send chemical readings and pictures via email, plus on-the-way notifications so you know when we’re arriving.
What services don’t we cover?
We focus on keeping your pool clean and swim-ready—no distractions. We do not handle major equipment repair/replacement (pump replacement, heaters), plumbing, leak detection/repair, or pool construction/resurfacing. If you need those, we’ll recommend a trusted specialist.
What is water polishing?
It’s a premium clarity process for that “sparkle” look—fine-tuned chemistry (like clarifier as needed), optimized filtration, and extra detail cleaning to remove micro-particles that cause dull or hazy water.
