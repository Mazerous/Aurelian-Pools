About Aurelian Pools

Built on discipline, pride, and consistency.

Hi, I’m Austin Molketin, the owner and operator of Aurelian Pools. I graduated from the University of South Florida (USF) in 2024 with a degree in Computer Science. As AI rapidly reshaped the industry and opportunities narrowed, I made a decision: pivot, work harder, and build something I could stand behind.

This business is personal. When you hire Aurelian Pools, you’re not getting a random tech— you’re getting an owner who shows up, communicates, and treats your home with respect. I don’t cut corners. I take care of your pool like it’s my own.

FAQ

Quick answers.

What’s included in “equipment checks”?
Each visit includes a visual + functional check of your pump, filter pressure, timers, and obvious leaks. If something looks off, you’ll get a clear explanation and a recommendation before any repair work.
What is water polishing?
It’s a premium clarity process for that “sparkle” look—using fine-tuned chemistry (like clarifier as needed), optimized filtration, and detail cleaning to remove micro-particles that cause dull or hazy water.
Do you bring chemicals?
This template assumes chemistry management is included. You can decide whether to bill chemicals separately or include an allowance—just update your wording before going live.
How do I start service?
